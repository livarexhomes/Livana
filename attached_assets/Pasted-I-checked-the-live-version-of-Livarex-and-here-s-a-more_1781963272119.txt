🔴 Major Issues I'd Fix First
1. "Coming Soon" Is Everywhere

Under cities:

Abuja
Port Harcourt
Kano
Ibadan
Enugu
Benin City

all display "Coming Soon".

Why this hurts

Visitors may assume:

the platform is unfinished
listings don't exist outside Lagos
the company is still in development
Better option

Instead of:

Coming Soon

Use:

Launching Soon
Join Waitlist

or collect leads from interested users.

2. Statistics Need Credibility

Homepage currently shows:

307+ Properties
108+ Landlords
10K+ Happy Tenants
850+ Landlords

The numbers appear inconsistent because landlord counts differ in different sections.

Fix

Use one source of truth.

Example:

307 Properties
108 Verified Landlords
1,250 Active Users

Real numbers build more trust than inflated numbers.

3. Property Search Is Too Basic

Current filters show:

Location
Type
Beds/Baths
Price

That's okay, but modern users expect:

State
Area
Property condition
Furnished/Unfurnished
Short-let
Pet friendly
Parking availability

The search experience can become a major competitive advantage.

4. Missing "How It Works"

A first-time visitor may wonder:

How exactly do I contact landlords directly?

Add a simple section:

Step 1

Search properties

Step 2

View verified landlord

Step 3

Chat on WhatsApp

Step 4

Schedule inspection

This reduces uncertainty.

5. Homepage Feels Too Short

After:

Hero
Listings
Cities
Testimonials

the page ends quickly.

Consider adding:

Featured properties
Why verification matters
Landlord onboarding
FAQ section
Market insights preview
🔴 Technical Issues To Verify

I couldn't inspect your code directly, but based on your earlier GitHub issues and development discussions, check these carefully:

Property URLs

Bad:

/property?id=123

Better:

/lagos/lekki/3-bedroom-duplex

SEO improves dramatically.

Missing Sitemap

Make sure:

/sitemap.xml

exists.

Missing Robots File

Make sure:

/robots.txt

exists.

Open Graph Images

When a property is shared on WhatsApp, users should see:

Property image
Price
Location

instead of a generic site preview.

🟠 Conversion Problems
No Strong Landlord CTA

You want inventory growth.

The homepage should prominently include:

List Your Property Free

or

above the fold.

No Urgency

Property platforms convert better when users see:

or

Recently listed

or

Inspection slots available
