STARTER STATS YOU SHOULD MAINTAIN

I’ll break it into 3 layers so you don’t overcomplicate it.

🟢 1. CORE PLATFORM STATS (Show on Homepage)

These are the only ones you should display publicly for now:

✅ Listings
Total Properties

👉 Example:

47+ Properties

✅ Landlords
Verified Landlords

👉 Example:

12+ Verified Landlords

✅ Requests (VERY IMPORTANT)
Property Requests Made

👉 Example:

83+ Requests Processed

⚠️ Optional (only if real)
Successful Matches / Rentals

👉 Example:

15+ Successful Rentals

👉 These 3–4 stats are enough.

Do NOT add:

10K users ❌
1000 landlords ❌
fake growth ❌
🟡 2. INTERNAL STATS (Your Dashboard)

These are more important than homepage stats.

👤 User Behavior

Track:

Total users
Active users (daily/weekly)
Properties viewed
Properties saved
🏠 Property Activity
Views per property
Requests per property
Most viewed listings
Least performing listings
📩 Request System (CRITICAL for your model)

Since you control interaction:

Track:

Requests Sent
Requests Approved
Requests Rejected
Inspections Scheduled
Deals Completed

👉 This is your real business engine

🔵 3. LANDLORD STATS (Retention Tool)

This is how you keep landlords on your platform.

Show them:

👀 Views
📩 Requests received
📅 Inspections booked
✅ Successful deals

👉 If landlords see results, they stay.

🚨 WHAT YOU SHOULD NOT SHOW YET

Avoid these for now:

“Happy tenants” (unless real)
“Downloads”
“Users served”
“Market insights numbers”

👉 These are easy to fake and easy to doubt.

💡 SMART TRICK (Make Small Numbers Look Strong)

Instead of:

12 landlords

Say:

12+ Verified Landlords in Lagos

Instead of:

15 rentals

Say:

15+ Successful Home Matches

👉 Same number, stronger perception.

🔥 BEST COMBINATION FOR LIVAREX (RIGHT NOW)

If I were you, I’d display ONLY this:

🏠 40+ Verified Properties
👤 12+ Verified Landlords
📩 80+ Property Requests Processed

That’s clean, believable, and aligned with your system.

⚠️ FINAL ADVICE (THIS IS IMPORTANT)

Your strength is not big numbers.

Your strength is:

Trust + Process

So your stats should reflect:

real activity
real usage
real progress
